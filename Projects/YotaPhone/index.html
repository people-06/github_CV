What we do

Yota Devices is a private international company dedicated to developing and producing high-tech consumer LTE devices such as smartphones, modems and routers. YotaPhone, the world’s first dual-screen, always-on smartphone developed by YotaDevices, is now on sale in 20 markets in Europe, the CIS and Middle East. Yota Devices sold more than 3 million 4G connectivity devices since its first products went on the market in 2009. The company’s headquarters are in Moscow with offices in Finland, Singapore and Germany.
